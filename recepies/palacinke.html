Palacinke

How to make this:

Ingredients for 10-ish palacinke:

  • 2 eggs
  • flour 200g
  • mineral water 500ml
  • vanilla
  • sugar (to be added as needed)
  • a pinch of salt
  • a small spoonful of baking powder

Preparation:

  1. Separate the egg whites from the yolk and mix it until it turns quite foamy, add a pinch of salt, and the vanilla. Once there, add the yolks to the mix as well.
  2. Add the half liter of the mineral water, followed by flour (which needs to be sieved), sugar to taste, and the spoonful of baking powder. Keep mixing it all in.
  3. Once you have the mix well mixed (without any lumps, and dense but liquid), pre-heat you pan (use butter or lard)
  4. Using the laddle, spread the mix over a hot pan, and after a minute of two, flip the palackina on the oter side
  5. Apply/spread whatever you want in your palacinka, roll it, and enjoy!
  6. Whoever didn't help in the making will be involved in the cleaning afterwards :D
